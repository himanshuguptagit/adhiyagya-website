Cause

Mission

To work towards a brighter future of the children of India, empowering them through education and creating opportunities for them to live a self-made dignified life.

Focus Areas

  • Personality Development
  • Discipline
  • Moral Values
  • Nationalism
  • Career Guidance
  • Extra-Curricular
  • Health Awareness
  • Societal & Environmental Awareness

Projects

Chalo School Chalein:

The parents of children who are not enrolled into schools are encouraged to enroll their children into schools so that these children can attain education.

Building the Foundation:

An initiative where the gap between “What should be taught” and “What is actually taught” is bridged. It involves working with school going children to strengthen their basic knowledge about subjects so that they may progress in their overall studies.

Educational Sponsorships:

Sponsorships to be arranged to bright children from economically weaker sections of the society in order to enable them to continue with their education and excel in it.

Vocational Training:

An initiative where vocational skills will be imparted to children who due to dire circumstances cannot study further in order to make them independent and confident.

Homework Scheme:

A platform where under privileged children of the society are provided tuitions to cope up with their studies. A platform where under privileged children of the society are provided tuitions to cope up with their studies.

Career Counseling:

An initiative where the children are provided guidance pertaining to various career options. It is also an attempt to encourage them to pursue higher education and not forego education due to lack of awareness.
